Starting Ecom.

Starting an e-commerce business is often romanticized as easy—a few clicks to a Shopify store and the money rolls in. The reality is that starting ecom is not about the technology; it's about foundation. Success isn't found in a trend, but in identifying a core market need and solving it better than anyone else.

This path requires extraordinary patience. The metrics you see posted online are often the culmination of months, sometimes years, of unseen failure. You must fall in love with the process and the data, not just the potential payout.

The marketplace demands hospitality in every interaction. In digital commerce, hospitality isn't just about good customer service; it's about an integrated philosophy. Every detail builds trust.

Finally, your mental state is the definitive variable. Developing a resilient mental state is critical. To build a store that demands to be seen, you must first do the work that remains invisible.
